Please paste the full content of your codebook into this file.

The `embed-codebook.ts` script will read this file to create the vector embeddings and store them in your Supabase database.

Example of content structure:

Variable: cntry
Label: Country
Description: This variable indicates the country in which the survey was conducted.
Values:
AT: Austria
BE: Belgium
...

Variable: ppltrst
Label: Most people can be trusted or you can't be too careful
Description: Generally speaking, would you say that most people can be trusted, or that you can't be too careful in dealing with people? 0 means you can't be too careful and 10 means that most people can be trusted.
